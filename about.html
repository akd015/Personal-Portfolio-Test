My Story

Hello! I'm Adinath Komanthakkal Deepkumar, a passionate web developer and designer with a love for creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. Currently pursuing my Computer Science degree at Christ University, Bangalore. I combine academic knowledge with hands-on project experience.

My journey in web development started three years ago when I built my first website for a local business. What began as a simple HTML page sparked a fascination with the intersection of technology and design. Since then, I've been constantly learning new technologies, experimenting with different frameworks, and refining my craft.

I'm particularly interested in user experience design and how small details can make a big difference in how people interact with digital products. My goal is to bridge the gap between beautiful design and functional code.
